Market Entry & Business Growth

Technolyze Sdn Bhd

Problem

Technolyze aimed to establish and grow its presence in Malaysia’s healthcare market but faced multiple challenges: complex regulatory requirements (MDA approval), lack of local market knowledge, need for structured sales operations, and importation coordination across multiple countries.

Solution

NADI Aksis provided end-to-end support including MDA regulatory registration, product importation from China, India, Turkey, and Italy, market entry strategy, sales team setup, and hands-on execution support.

“NADI Aksis has been instrumental in establishing and growing our business in Malaysia. Their expertise and hands-on approach make them a trusted strategic partner.”

RM100,000+ revenue within 4 months

Accelerated market penetration
